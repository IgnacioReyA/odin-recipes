Avocado Toast

Close Up

I used to eat this everyday, but now I don't have much time to make it. However, it's still delicous.

This is honestly quiet simple to make but very tasty.

Ingredients

  • 1 Bread
  • 2 Egg
  • 1 Avocado

Steps

  1. Fry eggs.
  2. Toast bread.
  3. Turn avocado into guacamole.
  4. Spread guacamole in toast.
  5. Add fried eggs.
