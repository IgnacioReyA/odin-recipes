Granola with Yogurt

Close Up

This is what I eat almost every day. It's simple, yet delicous and healthy.

I would have liked to make a more sophisticated recipe but perhaps another day.

Ingredients

  • Yogurt
  • Granola
  • Strawberries

Steps

  1. Add yogurt to a bowl.
  2. Add granola to bowl.
  3. Cut strawberries and add to bowl.
