Mousse

Close Up

This recipe is the best for a chocolate mousse. Never have I ever tried a mousse as good as the one you are going to make with this recipe.

This recipe I found it in a cooking book of Pablo Massay. He is an incredible chef with so much talent.

Ingredients

  • 6 eggs
  • 200 grams of chocolate
  • 100 grams of sugar
  • Spoon of butter

Steps

  1. Separate the 6 egg yolks from the egg white.
  2. Add the egg white to a blender until it becomes meringue, while adding 50 grams of sugar.
  3. Melt the 200 grams of chocolate with the butter in a Bain Marie while mixing.
  4. Add the 6 egg yolks with the other 50 grams of sugar in a Bain Marie while mixing up until the egg yolks are more yellowish and less viscous (Be sure the egg yolk doesn't cook).
  5. Mix the melted chocolate with the egg yolks.
  6. Mix the previous with half of the meringue.
  7. Now mix with the other half of the meringue, however it must be mixed in an enveloping way.
  8. Leave the mix in the refrigerator for 8 hours.
